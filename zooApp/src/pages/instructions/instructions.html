How to Play

To play, first make sure location services for this app are enabled. Then, you can start the adventure. Here is how the game works:

  1. The app will give you a hint leading you to a random animal in the zoo. Your job is to figure out which animal that is, and then find it in the zoo.
    Note: If you think the clue is too hard or cannot find the animal, you can click "Get another clue."
  2. When you are within a certain distance from the animal at the zoo, click submit location on the app to guess the location. If you are wrong, you will be prompted to try again.
  3. When you get the correct location and animal, you will recieve a congratulatory statement and then be able to view more pictures of the animal. Then, you can continue the hunt with another hint.
